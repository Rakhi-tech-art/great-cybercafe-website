About Great Cyber Café

Fast. Friendly. Reliable Digital Services Since 2024

Our Story

Established in 2024 by Pooja Arvind Jaiswal, Great Cyber Café was born with a simple vision: to offer fast, reliable, and honest digital services in the heart of Diva East, Thane. We are now a trusted name in the community for Aadhar, PAN, passport, online forms, printing, and computer education.

We believe that access to digital services should be affordable and available to everyone — students, job seekers, local families, and business owners.

Founder & Owner

Pooja Arvind Jaiswal

Founder & Owner, Great Cyber Café

Pooja Arvind Jaiswal, a BAF graduate, started Great Cyber Café in 2024 with a dream to help people with all kinds of digital services — right here in Diva East. Her goal was simple: to make internet and computer services easy, quick, and affordable for everyone in the community.

As a young and passionate businesswoman, Pooja focuses on friendly service, clear support, and reliable work. Whether you need to browse the internet, fill out government forms, or print important documents, she and her team are always ready to help with a smile. At Great Cyber Café, every customer is treated with care and respect.
